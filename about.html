About the Band

Who We Are

Sound Shallows is a genre‑fluid music project blending industrial grit, classical motifs, darkwave synths, grunge energy, and ambient calm. The project thrives on contrast — harshness and serenity, structure and chaos, digital and organic.

Influences

Classical composers, industrial pioneers, synthwave aesthetics, 90s grunge, and modern electronic production.
