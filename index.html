Welcome

Sound Shallows is an independent electronic project exploring time, identity, and the quiet fear of being forgotten, along with the small rebellions we make against disappearing, even when the struggle is with ourselves.

Built from piano, distortion, and slow-burning tension, the music moves between warmth and weight, comfort and collapse.

Each release is part of a larger narrative. It is not just a track but a moment, a reflection on aging, memory, and the space between who we were, who we are, and who we refuse to stop becoming.
